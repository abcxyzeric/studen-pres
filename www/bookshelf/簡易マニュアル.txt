##About this manual
◆A manual that highlights the fundamental knowledge 
　of this game.


##Basic Controls
◆Moving
　Directional keys: Movement (Diagonal movement allowed)
　Z・Enter: Speak, Confirm
　X・Esc: Cancel, Main Menu
　Shift: Toggle Dash.

◆In conversation
　Direction keys: Cursor movement
　Z・Enter: Confirm, Send Message
　X・Esc: Cancel, Send Message

◆Easy Battle (Action Battle)
　Z・Enter: Attack, hold to charge attack.

For more details, please refer to the \c[3]"Keyboard Settings"\c[0] 
and \c[3]"Gamepad Settings"\c[0] in the configuration menu.

##Advanced Operations
◆Moving
　D: HP Recovery
　S: Change Direction
　C: Jump (only effective in certain scenes)

◆In Conversation
　A: Auto-scrolling.
　W Ctrl: Skip
　Q: Message History
　Shift: Clear Message Window

◆Anytime
　F2: Display FPS value.
　F4: Toggle Full-screen.
　F5: Forcefully restart the game.
　Alt + F4: Force quit the game.
