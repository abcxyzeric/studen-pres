#

##Battle Voices
◆Play Rio's voice during battles 
　and when entering dungeons.

　This setting is for configuring 
　the main menu screen in the game.
　You can change it from the settings. 
　Cannot be changed from  the environment settings 
　on the title screen.

##Always Dash
◆ Always dash while moving.
　ON: Dash with the directional keys.
　Hold down the 'Shift' key to walk.
　
　OFF: Walk using the arrow keys.
　Pressing the 'Shift' key while moving allows you to dash.

##Diagonal Movement
◆While moving, 
　you can move vertically, horizontally and diagonally.
　
　ON: Movement in 8 directions.

　OFF: Movement in 4 directions.

##Command Memory
◆Remembers the cursor position during command selection.
　It is set to OFF by default.

##Launch in full screen.
◆Full screen mode upon game startup
　It will become (borderless window).

　The settings will be applied 
　from the next time you start up.

##Various Voices
◆ Adjust various volumes.

　Overall Volume: All sounds.

　BGM Volume: Music.

　BGS Volume: Music during H scenes.

　ME Volume: Jingle for resting on the home bed.

　SE Volume: Sound Effects.

　Voice Volume: Voices of Rio Osakai and Mai Higuchi.
　　　　This is also the volume of the battle voices.

##Gamepad Settings
◆Change gamepad button assignment.

##Keyboard Settings
◆Change keyboard key assignments.

##Convenient Button (During Conversation)
◆Display the following buttons during conversation.

　Auto: Message auto-forwarding.
　Skip: Skip message.
　Log: Reviewing messages.
　Voice: Enable voice playback.

##Convenience Button (During H Scene)
◆Display the following buttons during the H scene.

　Auto: Automatic message forwarding.
　Skip: Skip message.
　Log: Message review.
　Voice: Play the voice.
　Config: Open settings.

##Displaying Character Graphic (Moving)
◆Set the opacity of the character sprite during movement.

　Not displayed, Displayed lightly, Displayed heavily
　You can decide the degree of display like this.

##Character Illustration Animation (Moving)
◆Animate the standing picture during movement.

　When the transparency of the character illustration 
　is set to "Not Visible".
　It will not appear.

　Also, there is some additional load when animating.
　Please be careful.

## Erotic Art Anime
◆In the animation-enabled H scene,
　animate the images.

　Depending on the performance and specifications 
　of your device, the animation may be accurate.
　There may be times when it is not always displayed.

　Also, please be aware that there may be 
　some additional burden.

##The character illustration does not blink.
◆During conversations, Rio and Mai will blink.

　ON: No blinking.

　OFF: Blinking

##Cross-section of Erotic Art
◆ Display a cross-sectional view in the H scene 
　with cross-sectional view support.

##Cutscene of Erotic Art Image
◆During the H scene, 
　various effects such as screen scrolling will be performed.

　This effect is separate from the animation.

Skip the same H scene.
◆ Automatically skip previously viewed H scenes.

　Even when turned OFF, 
　the option to skip will still be displayed.

##Other Voices
◆Play the voices of Rio and Mai 
  (excluding battle voices).

　ON: Enable playback.

　OFF: Disable playback.

　It is set to ON by default.

##Inflict damage on the enemy with a physical attack.
◆When you collide with an enemy in the dungeon,
　both the enemy and yourself will take damage.

　When set to OFF, only you will take damage.

##Location Name Display
◆Display the name of the location 
　when entering towns or dungeons.

##Minimap
◆Display a mini-map on the bottom left of the screen.

　There are also areas where some parts of 
　the mini-map are not displayed.

##Boss Difficulty
◆Changing the difficulty level of the boss battle.

　There are achievements related to difficulty levels, 
　but they can be obtained later.
